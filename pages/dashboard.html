Psychology of Money

What percentage of people do you think make purchases due to FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)?

FOMO, or Fear of Missing Out, is when you feel anxious or left out because you think everyone else is having fun or doing something exciting, and you’re not part of it. It's like seeing your friends post pictures from a party or a cool event online and feeling like you’re missing out on something important or fun.

Answer: 60% of people make purchases because of FOMO, mostly within 24 hours.
